Why Kato.8 exists

Kato.8 Studios is built around a simple belief: sustainability comes from shipping. The studio focuses on small, achievable milestones that build real execution credibility — without overextending scope or relying entirely on speculative funding.

What we optimize for

  • Clear milestones and measurable progress.
  • Focused scope and disciplined iteration.
  • Repeatable production habits that scale responsibly.

What you should expect

  • Simple language (no smoke, no fluff).
  • Updates mapped to deliverables.
  • Funding conversations tied to specific milestones.

FAQ

Questions people usually ask

Short answers, no fluff.

Why start with Hotdog Hustler?

It’s designed to be shipped in small phases — which makes progress predictable, testable, and easier to fund. Shipping creates leverage for the next project.

What does “milestone funding” look like?

A defined milestone, acceptance criteria, and a tangible output (build/video/update). Funding maps to the deliverable — not vague time.

How do you keep scope under control?

Lock the core loop early, ship increments, and only expand when stability is proven and value is clear.

Where should someone start if they want to help?

Start with the Updates page to understand current priorities, then use Contact to propose a milestone you’d like to support.

Want to support a milestone?

If you’re interested in funding or collaborating, reach out with what you want to support and what “done” should look like.
